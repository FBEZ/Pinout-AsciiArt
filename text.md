#74HC595
=======

*Description*: 8-bit serial in/out Shift Register 3-State Outputs

*Datasheet*: http://www.ti.com/lit/ds/symlink/sn74hc595.pdf

*Pinout*: 


```
   +--------+
QB |[1]	[16]|VCC
QC |[2]	[15]|QA
QD |[3]	[14]|SER
QE |[4]	[13]|~{OE}
QF |[5]	[12]|RCLK
QG |[6]	[11]|SRCLK
QH |[7]	[10]|~{SRCLR}
GND|[8]	 [9]|QH'
   +--------+
```
